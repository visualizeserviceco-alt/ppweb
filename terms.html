Terms & Conditions

Professional standards and policies for Paps Productions photography sessions

Effective Date: October 20, 2025

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

1. Booking & Payments

  • All sessions must be booked in advance through the official Paps Productions booking system or direct contact via Instagram.
  • A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required to secure your booking and reserve your session date.
  • Remaining balances are due before or at the start of the session via cash, Venmo, or agreed payment method.
  • No session will proceed without full payment confirmation.

2. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of the session will result in forfeiture of the deposit.
  • Rescheduling is allowed once per booking if requested at least 48 hours in advance at no additional cost.
  • Weather-related cancellations initiated by Paps Productions will be rescheduled without penalty.
  • Repeated cancellations or last-minute reschedules may result in restrictions on future bookings.

3. Photographer Rights & Creative Control

  • Paps Productions reserves the right to refuse service to anyone who is disrespectful, unprofessional, or disruptive to the creative process.
  • The photographer maintains full creative control over composition, lighting, editing style, and overall artistic direction.
  • Raw or unedited photos are never provided under any circumstance — only professionally edited finals are delivered.
  • The photographer may terminate a session immediately if client behavior is unsafe, threatening, or violates these terms — with no refund provided.

4. Client Conduct & Expectations

  • Clients are expected to arrive on time with vehicles and/or models prepared as discussed during the pre-session consultation.
  • Disrespect, harassment, or aggressive behavior toward the photographer or any team members will result in immediate session termination.
  • The photographer's creative process should be respected — constructive feedback is welcome, but excessive direction during shooting should be minimal.
  • Clients should come prepared with any specific shots or concepts discussed beforehand to maximize session efficiency.

5. Delivery & Post-Production

  • Standard delivery time for edited photos is 24–72 hours for photo sessions, depending on package size and complexity.
  • Video sessions (roller content) may take up to 5–7 days for full editing and delivery.
  • Additional edits or major revisions beyond the agreed package scope will incur extra fees as discussed.
  • Paps Productions retains full discretion over which images are selected and edited for final delivery based on quality and artistic standards.

6. Copyright & Usage Rights

  • All images and videos remain the intellectual property of Paps Productions.
  • Clients receive a personal and promotional license for non-commercial use (social media, personal portfolio, etc.).
  • Commercial use (advertisements, resale, sponsorships, merchandise, business marketing) requires a separate licensing agreement and additional fees.
  • Credit to Paps Productions (@paps_productions) is greatly appreciated when sharing content online and helps support my work.

7. Weather, Environment & Equipment Safety

  • The photographer may reschedule a session due to unsafe weather conditions or environmental factors that could damage equipment or compromise safety.
  • Clients should not handle, touch, or operate camera gear, lighting, or other professional equipment without explicit permission.
  • Location shoots may require additional considerations for permits, parking, or access — clients will be informed of any special requirements beforehand.

8. Refund & Deposit Policy

  • Deposits are non-refundable under all circumstances as they secure your booking slot and cover pre-session planning time.
  • Full refunds are only issued if Paps Productions must cancel a session due to unforeseen personal emergencies, equipment failure, or other technical reasons beyond client control.
  • Partial services (if session is ended early due to client conduct) do not qualify for refunds.

9. Privacy & Portfolio Usage

  • All client contact information, personal details, and project specifics are kept strictly confidential and never shared with third parties.
  • Paps Productions may use selected images for portfolio, website, and promotional purposes unless the client specifically requests complete privacy in writing before the session.
  • Clients who prefer complete privacy should communicate this during booking — privacy requests will be honored with no questions asked.

10. Agreement & Contact

By booking a session with Paps Productions, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to all terms outlined above.

Questions about these terms? Feel free to reach out via Instagram (@paps_productions) before booking your session.

These terms ensure a professional, creative, and mutually respectful experience for all parties involved. Thank you for choosing Paps Productions for your automotive photography needs.
